📚 What You'll Learn

You already know that variables are letters representing numbers. Now we're leveling up to handle complex expressions with multiple variables, operations, and terms.

Think of algebraic expressions as recipes where ingredients can be combined, quantities can be adjusted, and the final result depends on what you put in!

Key Vocabulary:

📌 Term: A number, variable, or product of both (like 5, x, or 3y)

📌 Coefficient: The number multiplying a variable (in 7x, the coefficient is 7)

📌 Like Terms: Terms with the same variable part (2x and 5x are like terms)

📌 Constant: A term without a variable (just a number like 8)

💡 Core Concept: Combining Like Terms

What Are Like Terms?

Like terms have the exact same variable part. You can only combine terms that are "alike."

Examples of Like Terms:
  • 3x and 7x (both have x)
  • 5y² and -2y² (both have y²)
  • 9 and 4 (both are constants)
NOT Like Terms:
  • 2x and 2y (different variables)
  • 3x and 3x² (different powers)
  • 5xy and 5x (different variable parts)

How to Combine Like Terms

Think of it like organizing your inventory in a game:

You have: 3 health potions + 5 mana potions + 2 health potions

Organize: (3 + 2) health potions + 5 mana potions = 5 health potions + 5 mana potions

Same with algebra: 3x + 5y + 2x = (3x + 2x) + 5y = 5x + 5y

🎨 Visual: Combining Like Terms

Add the coefficients of like terms: (4 + 3)x + (2 + 5)y = 7x + 7y

✏️ Example 1: Simplifying Complex Expressions

Problem: Simplify: 6a + 3b - 2a + 8b + 5

Solution:

  1. Identify like terms:
    • Terms with a: 6a and -2a
    • Terms with b: 3b and 8b
    • Constants: 5
  2. Group like terms together:

    = (6a - 2a) + (3b + 8b) + 5

  3. Combine each group:

    = 4a + 11b + 5

Answer: 4a + 11b + 5

💡 Key Insight: When subtracting a variable (like -2a), you subtract the coefficients: 6 - 2 = 4, so 6a - 2a = 4a.

✏️ Example 2: Distributive Property

Problem: A streaming service charges $12 per month, but if you refer friends, you get $3 off per friend. If you refer f friends, write and simplify an expression for 6 months of service.

Solution:

  1. Cost per month with f friends:

    Monthly cost = 12 - 3f

  2. Cost for 6 months:

    Total = 6(12 - 3f)

  3. Apply distributive property (multiply 6 by each term):

    = 6 × 12 - 6 × 3f

    = 72 - 18f

Answer: 72 - 18f dollars

💡 Real Meaning: If you refer 2 friends (f = 2): 72 - 18(2) = 72 - 36 = $36 for 6 months. That's $30 in savings!

🎮 Quick Check: Translate and Simplify!

Question: You're buying in-game currency. Gold coins cost x gems each, and you buy 5 of them. Silver coins cost y gems each, and you buy 3 of them. Then you find a treasure chest with 2 more gold coins and 4 silver coins. Write and simplify an expression for the total coins.

Answer: 7 gold + 7 silver (or 7x + 7y if counting gems)

Solution:

Initial purchase: 5 gold + 3 silver

Treasure chest: 2 gold + 4 silver

Total coins = (5 + 2) gold + (3 + 4) silver

= 7 gold + 7 silver

Or in gems: 5x + 3y + 2x + 4y = 7x + 7y gems spent

📝 Practice Questions

Question 1 (Easy): Simplify: 7m + 4m

Answer: 11m

These are like terms (both have m), so add the coefficients: 7 + 4 = 11.

Question 2 (Medium): Simplify: 9x + 5 - 3x + 2

Answer: 6x + 7

Step-by-step:

Group like terms: (9x - 3x) + (5 + 2)

= 6x + 7

Question 3 (Medium): Apply the distributive property: 4(2x + 5)

Answer: 8x + 20

Step-by-step:

= 4 × 2x + 4 × 5

= 8x + 20

Question 4 (Hard): Simplify: 3(2x + 1) + 2(x - 4)

Answer: 8x - 5

Step-by-step:

= 3(2x) + 3(1) + 2(x) + 2(-4)

= 6x + 3 + 2x - 8

= (6x + 2x) + (3 - 8)

= 8x - 5

Question 5 (Hard): Write an expression for: "Five times a number n, decreased by twice the sum of n and 8, then simplified."

Answer: 3n - 16

Step-by-step translation:

"Five times a number n" = 5n

"The sum of n and 8" = n + 8

"Twice the sum" = 2(n + 8)

"Decreased by" means subtract: 5n - 2(n + 8)

Simplify:

= 5n - 2n - 16

= 3n - 16

📌 Quick Summary

  • Like terms have the same variable part and can be combined by adding/subtracting coefficients
  • A coefficient is the number in front of a variable (in 7x, it's 7)
  • Distributive property: a(b + c) = ab + ac (multiply outside term by each inside term)
  • To simplify expressions: combine like terms and apply the distributive property
  • Constants (plain numbers) are like terms with each other

🌍 Real-World Connection: How Video Games Calculate Damage

Ever notice how complex damage formulas are in games? Here's a simplified version from a popular RPG:

Damage Formula:

Base Damage = 50

Weapon Bonus = 3w (where w is weapon level)

Strength Bonus = 2s (where s is strength stat)

Critical Multiplier = 1.5 for crits, 1 for normal hits

For a normal hit with weapon level 10 and strength 25:

Damage = 1(50 + 3(10) + 2(25))

= 50 + 30 + 50

= 130 damage!

Game developers create thousands of these formulas to balance gameplay. That's why understanding expressions and variables is essential for game design!

🚀 Challenge Problem: The YouTube Revenue Formula

A YouTuber's monthly revenue comes from multiple sources:

  • Ad revenue: $4 per 1000 views
  • Sponsorships: $500 per sponsor
  • Memberships: $5 per member
  • Platform fee: 30% of total revenue is taken by YouTube

Part A: If the channel gets v thousand views, has s sponsors, and m members, write an expression for total revenue BEFORE the platform fee.

Part B: Write an expression for revenue AFTER the 30% platform fee (YouTuber keeps 70%).

Part C: If the channel had 500,000 views (v = 500), 2 sponsors (s = 2), and 150 members (m = 150), how much does the YouTuber actually earn?

Hint for Part A: Add up all three revenue sources:

• Ad revenue = 4v (since v is in thousands of views)

• Sponsorships = 500s

• Memberships = 5m

Hint for Part B: If YouTube takes 30%, the creator keeps 70% = 0.7 × total

Part A Answer: 4v + 500s + 5m

Explanation: Sum all revenue sources before fees.

Part B Answer: 0.7(4v + 500s + 5m) or 2.8v + 350s + 3.5m

Simplified form:

0.7(4v + 500s + 5m)

= 0.7(4v) + 0.7(500s) + 0.7(5m)

= 2.8v + 350s + 3.5m

Part C Answer: $2,155

Solution:

= 2.8(500) + 350(2) + 3.5(150)

= 1,400 + 700 + 525

= $2,155 per month! 💰

That's why creators focus on multiple revenue streams, not just ad views!
