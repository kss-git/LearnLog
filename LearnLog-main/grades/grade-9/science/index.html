🌟 Why Chemistry Matters

Chemistry isn't just about test tubes and lab coats. It's literally everything:

  • Your Phone: Lithium-ion batteries powering every swipe
  • Cooking: Chemical reactions create flavor and texture
  • Medicine: Every medication is carefully designed chemistry
  • Environment: Understanding pollution and clean energy
  • Sports: Energy drinks, protein synthesis, muscle chemistry
