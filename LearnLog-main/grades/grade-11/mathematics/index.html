🎯 What You'll Master

  • Limits: The concept that makes calculus work - understanding behavior near a point
  • Derivatives: Finding instantaneous rates of change and slopes of curves
  • Optimization: Finding maximum and minimum values in real situations
  • Integration: Calculating areas, volumes, and accumulated quantities

💡 Why Calculus Matters

Calculus helps us understand and model the world around us:

  • Physics uses derivatives to describe motion and acceleration
  • Engineers use optimization to design efficient structures
  • Economists use calculus to maximize profit and minimize cost
  • Scientists use integration to calculate total amounts from rates
