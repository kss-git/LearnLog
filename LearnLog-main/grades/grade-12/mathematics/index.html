Advanced Calculus

Explore the most powerful tools in mathematics. Advanced calculus enables you to model change, optimize complex systems, and solve real-world problems in physics, economics, engineering, and data science. This course emphasizes applications and deep understanding.

Unit 1: Advanced Calculus

Lesson 1: Advanced Derivatives

Implicit differentiation, related rates, and higher-order derivatives with physics applications

Lesson 2: Integration Techniques

Integration by parts, partial fractions, trigonometric substitution, and numerical methods

Lesson 3: Differential Equations

First-order ODEs, separable equations, and applications in population dynamics and physics

Lesson 4: Calculus Applications

Optimization, curve sketching, physics models, and economic applications
