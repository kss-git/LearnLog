About This Course

Welcome to Grade 10 Science! This year, you'll dive deep into the fascinating world of cells and energy. Discover how your body converts food into energy, how plants harness sunlight, and how every living thing is built from microscopic factories called cells. This knowledge connects directly to health, fitness, nutrition, and understanding life itself!

Unit 1: Cell Biology

Discover the microscopic world inside all living things

Lesson 1: Cell Structure

Explore the organelles that power every cell in your body

Lesson 2: Cell Membrane Transport

Learn how nutrients enter cells and waste exits

Lesson 3: Cellular Respiration

Discover how cells convert food into usable energy

Lesson 4: Photosynthesis

Understand how plants capture sunlight and create food

Unit 2: Genetics and Heredity

Master the molecular basis of life and inheritance

Lesson 5: DNA Structure

Discover the double helix blueprint of all living things

Lesson 6: Inheritance Patterns

Learn how traits pass from parents to offspring using Punnett squares

Lesson 7: Genetic Mutations

Explore how changes in DNA cause disease, evolution, and diversity

Lesson 8: Genetic Engineering

See how CRISPR and GMO technology are changing medicine and agriculture
